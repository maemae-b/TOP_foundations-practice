Ginataang Monggo With Tofu And Malunggay

Description:

You might have heard of other variations of this dish. From pork to dried fish, you can really blend this with a lot of different ingredients. But if you haven’t tried it with some subtly savory fried tofu, and of course, the ever nutritious malunggay leaves, I highly suggest you do.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces mung beans
  • 1 Knorr Pork Cube
  • 40 grams Knorr Ginataang Gulay Mix
  • 8 ounces fried tofu diced
  • 1 cup malunggay leaves
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 to mato diced (optional)
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed
  • 6 cups water
  • 3 Tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Saute garlic, onion, and tomato.
  2. Add mung beans. Saute for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour water in the pot. Let boil.
  4. Add Knorr Pork Cube. Let boil. Continue cooking until the mung beans are mushy. Note: add more water as needed.
  5. Dilute Knorr Ginataang Gulay Mix in 1 ½ cups water. Mix well. Pour into the pot.
  6. Add fried tofu. Cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Put the malunggay leaves in the pot and then season with ground black pepper and salt.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve. Share and enjoy!
